My Watches Vintage Collection

I am a Hobbyist mainly interested in vintage watches from the 40s to end 60s. All my watches have been personally repaired/serviced. Still a lot to learn but happy to share with you my collection and equipment. Feel free to contact me to give any advice or feedback.
